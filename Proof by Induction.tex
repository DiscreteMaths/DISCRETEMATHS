\begin{frame}
\frametitle{Proof by Induction}
\Large
Let the summation $s_n$ be defined as follows:
\[s_n = 1 + 3 + 5 + \ldots + (2n - 1) \qquad \mbox{for n }\in \mathbb{Z}^{+}\]

Use the method of induction to prove that $s_n = n^2$ for all $n \geq 1$.
