\begin{frame}
\frametitle{Functions}
Evaluate $f(6)$ 


\[ f(x) = \lfloor \frac{x+1}{2} \rfloor \]


\[ f(6) = \lfloor \frac{\boldsymbol{6}+1}{2}\rfloor = \lfloor  \frac{7}{2}\rfloor\]


\[  \lfloor 3.5 \rfloor  = 3  \]
